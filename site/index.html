Project overview

Stay & Shop Reservation System

A sophisticated Java SE application that automates the reservation process for a combined store and hotel. The system provides a seamless experience where guests can book rooms and shop for products all in one integrated platform.

Hotel management

Three themed floors with various room types, quality levels, and comprehensive reservation management

Retail integration

Browse and purchase products during your stay with a seamless shopping cart experience

Roles & access

Support for hotel guests, clerks, and administrators with role-based access control

Billing & payments

Comprehensive billing system with combined hotel and shopping charges, cancellation policies, and corporate billing
